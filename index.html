Biography

Artist and musician based in Hanover, New Hampshire, originally from Chengdu, China.

Currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science and Digital Arts at Dartmouth College. Holds a Bachelor of Music in Computer Music from the Peabody Institute of the Johns Hopkins University.

Work focuses on computer music, algorithmic composition using SuperCollider, and developing 3D interactive environments with Unity and Maya.
